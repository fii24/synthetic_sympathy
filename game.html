Synthetic Sympathy

2075 C.E. After the Third Technological Revolution, androids came to populate the Earth. However, they are not the sentient, more-than-human androids of science ficton. They are a little more than advanced humanoid robots, with no sense of emotion or conscience beyond the black and white laws of their code. At least, they were...

Enter Unit 24707, the first android with empathy. Right now, she is in a testing phase, interacting with humans extensively as a high school receptionist.
